UWE Campus Resource Finder

Our team developed a mobile app prototype to assist University of the West of England (UWE) students in locating resources and spaces for their academic work across campus. The project followed a user-centered design approach and leveraged modern development tools.

View Research Documentation

Here are the key aspects of the project:

  1. User Research:

    • Created user personas and journey maps to understand student needs.

  2. Design Phase:

    • Used Figma for initial prototyping
    • Adhered to UWE's brand guidelines

  3. Data Collection:

    • Gathered information on campus spaces, including:
      • Opening times
      • Equipment availability
      • Location details

  4. Backend Development:

    • Utilised Supabase for data storage and management

  5. Frontend Development:

    • Transferred Figma designs to FlutterFlow
    • Integrated Supabase data into the app

The resulting prototype offers UWE students an efficient way to find and access campus resources, enhancing their academic experience.
